About Us

Founded in 2023, Blue Ocean Consulting is a Canada-based Overseas Educational Consultancy, committed to offering top-notch solutions for international students aspiring to study abroad. With a dedicated focus on guidance and counseling, BOC ensures that students receive the best support to pursue their educational dreams overseas. As an emerging leader in the field of overseas education services, Blue Ocean Consulting serves as a comprehensive one-stop solution for students' aspirations to study abroad, expertly matching their competencies and budget with the right institutions to help them achieve their desired career paths.

Blue Ocean Consulting excels in facilitating education opportunities in prestigious institutes across Australia, Canada, France, India, New Zealand, Singapore, the UK, and the USA. Recognizing the pivotal role of education in shaping students' careers, our experienced mentors are adept at resolving all doubts and dilemmas to help students make well-informed decisions. From course counseling and university selection to country selection, scholarships, education loans, pre-departure orientation, on-arrival support, and ongoing follow-ups during studies, our comprehensive assistance ensures a smooth journey from application to settling abroad. At Blue Ocean Consulting, we are dedicated to making students' dreams of studying overseas a reality.
